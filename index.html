The Fifty-Five Million Dollar Junkyard

The Fifty-Five Million Dollar Junkyard is a documentary short film directed, shot, and edited by Wesley Ward. The film follows Ward as he explores the town's scenic locations, set to narration by former resident Dan Ward, who reminisces about the town's troubles in the past, present, and future.
